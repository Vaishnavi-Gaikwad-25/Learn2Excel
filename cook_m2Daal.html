Enjoy Staple Indian Dal Dishes

Toor Dal

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pigeon pea lentils (toor dal)
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 7-8 curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds (Mohari)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds (Jeera seeds)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • 1 dry red chili (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Recipe:

  1. Rinse dal and soak for 15 minutes.
  2. Pressure cook dal with water, turmeric, and salt for 3 whistles on medium heat. Mash lightly.
  3. Heat ghee add hing, curry leaves, green chili, mustard seeds, cumin, garlic and red chili.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Now add the dal, top with coriander

Serves well with: Steamed rice, papad, and pickle.

Moong Dal

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yellow moong dal
  • 3 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 7-8 curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds (Mohari)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds (Jeera seeds)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • 1/2 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Recipe:

  1. Rinse dal and soak for 15 minutes.
  2. Pressure cook dal with water and salt for 3 whistles on medium heat. Mash lightly.
  3. Heat ghee add hing, curry leaves, green chili, mustard seeds, cumin, ginger and garlic.
  4. Now add the dal, top with coriander

Serves well with: Jeera rice or roti for a light meal.

Masoor Dal

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils (masoor dal)
  • 3 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 7-8 curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds (Mohari)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds (Jeera seeds)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Recipe:

  1. Rinse dal and soak for 15 minutes.
  2. Pressure cook dal with turmeric, salt, and water for 2 whistles.
  3. Heat the ghee, add mustard seeds, cumin, garlic, green chili, onion — sauté till golden.
  4. Add tomatoes, red chili powder — cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Now add the dal, top with coriander

Serves well with: Steamed rice, bhakri, or chapati.

Dal Tadka

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup pigeon pea lentils (toor dal)
  • 1/2 cup red lentils (masoor dal)
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds (Mohari)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds (Jeera seeds)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 2 dry red chilies
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Recipe:

  1. Wash both dals and soak for 15 minutes.
  2. Pressure cook turmeric, red chili powder, salt, and water for 3 whistles.
  3. Mash gently.
  4. Heat ghee for first tadka, add cumin, garlic, onion — sauté until golden.
  5. Add tomatoes, coriander powder, garam masala — cook until tomatoes are soft. Mix with dal.
  6. For final tadka, heat ghee, add cumin seeds, garlic slices, red chilies, red chili powder, and hing. Pour over dal.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot!

Serves well with: Jeera rice, naan, or tandoori roti.
